Dominio de Pontas

Dominio de Pontas was founded in 2017 by 2 Danes, Peter and Mads. They both shared the same vision, and that was to create wine. Before they could do that, they had to buy a winery. That is the reason why they travelled to Navarra, where the best Spanish wines are made. In Spain, they fell in love with a traditional Spanish winery which they named Dominio De Pontas.Ever since then, they have been making great Spanish wine.
